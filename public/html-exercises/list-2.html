Blueberry Cheesecake

Ingredients

    For crust
  • 2 1/3 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup sugar
      • For filling
  • 4-8 ounce packages cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 5 large eggs
  • 1/16 container sour cream>
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
      • For topping
  • 2 6-ounce baskets fresh blueberries
  • 1/3 cup all-fruit blueberry spread

    • Preparation

      Make crust
  • Position rack in center of oven and preheat to 375 F.
  • Wrap outside of 10-inch-diameter springform pan with heavy-duty foil.
  • Combine all ingredients in processor and blend using on/off turns until crumbs are mosit.
  • Press crumb mixture firmly onto bottom and 2 inches up sides of prepared pan.
  • Bake until crust begins to brown, about 8 minues, and transfer crust to rack and cool.
      • Make filling
  • Using electric mixer, beat cream cheese and sugar in large bowl until well blended.
  • Beat in flour and add eggs 1 at at time, beating just until combined.
  • Beat in sour cream, mil and vanilla.
  • Pour filling into crust.
  • Place springform pan in large roasting pan.
  • Pour enough hot water into roasting pan to come 1 inch up sides of pan.
  • Bake cheesecake until just set in center and top is slightly puffed and golden brown.
  • Turn off oven; keep door closed; let cheesecake stand in oven for 1 hour.
  • Remove cheesecake from roasting pan and refrigerate until cold.
  • Cover; refrigerate overnight.
      • Make topping
  • Stir blueberry spread in large skillet over low heat until melted.
  • Remove from heat and add blueberries.
  • Spoon blueberry mixture over top of cake, spreading evenly.
  • Chill until cold and transfer cake to platter.
